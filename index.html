1st Hunts (Hartford) Scout Group

We are a small but thriving Scout group that meets at Hartford Village Hall in Huntingdon (Cambridgeshire) on a Thursday evening. We currently run Beavers, Cubs and Scouts. We camp 2-3 times a year, and try to provide a varied activity schedule with a focus on the outdoors.
